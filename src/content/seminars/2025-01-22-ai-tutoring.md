## Abstract

The emergence of LLM-based learning assistants like ChatGPT has sparked intense debate in education. Are these tools helpful learning aids or crutches that prevent deep understanding?

This seminar will cover:

- Current research on AI tutoring effectiveness
- Case studies from VT courses
- Strategies for integrating AI tools thoughtfully
- Open questions for future research
